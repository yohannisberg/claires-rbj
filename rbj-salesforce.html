Jewelry Functionalities

INTERNALLY
THREADED

Has threading on the inside
of the post, with a screw mechanism on
the design side of the jewelry.

THREADLESS
FLATBACK

Instead of using threads, this design relies
on tension to secure the two pieces.
The design end features a thin pin that fits into
the post, and you bend the pin inside the post
to create the tension.

EXTERNALLY
THREADED

Has threading on the inside of the
design side of the jewelry, with the post serving
as the screw to connect the two pieces.

CLICKER
HOOP

The seamless design of the clasp
ensures
that the connection between the
two parts
is flush and smooth, with no visible
gaps,
creating a sleek and elegant finish.

HOW TO USE THREADLESS JEWELRY

Insert the earring post through the back of your ear. Once the post is visible at the front of your piercing, insert the pin slightly into the post and follow the below steps:

  • Bend the pin to create tension. *Be cautious not to bend it too sharply, as this may prevent you from adjusting the fit later.*
  • Once the pin is bent, pull the top out from the post,
    then re-insert and push the pin all the way into the post.
    Tip: If it's too tight, adjust the bend for a more comfortable fit.

To Remove: Hold front and back of earring with either hand, gently pull the pin out from the post and remove the post from the back of your ear.

How to Wear Threaded Flatbacks

Insert the earring post through the back of your ear.
Once the post is visible at the front of your piercing,
attach the design top to the post and follow the below steps:

  • Hold the earring with both handsone hand on the base of the post, and the other on the design top.
  • Screw the design top by rotating it counterclockwise.

To Remove: Hold earring with both hands and unscrew top from post, once disconnected remove post from the back of your ear.

How to USE CLICKER HOOPS

The seamless design of the clasp ensures that the connection between the two parts is flush and smooth, with no visible gaps, creating a sleek and elegant finish.

  • Gently open the hinged segment of the hoop by pulling it upwards.
  • Slide the open part of the hoop through your piercing, ensuring the open segment is on the outside.
  • Close the hoop by pressing the two sides together until you hear a distinct "click," indicating that it is securely fastened.

To Remove: Locate the hinges on hoop, then with both hands hold hoop in place, pull the hinged segment upwards and slide hoop out of your ear.
